Learning The BCG values

The first thing I have done during my onboarding week is learn the core BCG values. They really resonated with me and I intend to apply them in my daily life.

Integrity

Integrity is the first essential value in BCG, and I really like that. It means that I can expect transparency and honesty from everyone around me, and my other fellow BCGers can expect the same from me.

Respect

Everyone is treated with respect at BCG, and I could notice that from my first week. Everyone is nice and respectful.

Diversity

This is by far my favorite core BCG value, BCG embraces diversity which means that I can learn from a plethora of people with different backgrounds and cultures.

I was able to meet really inspiring people.

During my first two weeks, I had the opprtunity to meet awesome people, with awesome experiences, and awesome brains. I befriended quickly some of them, and BCG really gave us a very warm welcome, and I'm really grateful for that.
